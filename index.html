📖 How to Play

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. 1Look at the atoms on screen — each shows its element symbol and how many bonds it needs.
  2. 2Click or drag from one atom to another to create a covalent bond (shared electron pair).
  3. 3Follow valence rules: H=1, O=2, N=3, C=4. Never exceed an atom's valence!
  4. 4Complete the molecule by satisfying all atoms' valence requirements.
  5. 5The round ends when every atom glows green — all bonds correct!

💡 Game Tips

  • ⚡Each bond = 2 shared electrons between atoms.
  • ⚡Count the valence electrons — atoms want a full outer shell (octet rule)!
  • ⚡Oxygen needs 2 bonds — it forms water (H₂O), not HO!
  • ⚡Carbon needs 4 bonds — it's the foundation of all organic chemistry.
  • ⚡Lone pairs (unbonded electrons) are shown as dots on atoms — they don't form bonds.
  • ⚡Double bonds (=) count as 2 bonds for each atom. Triple bonds (≡) count as 3.
